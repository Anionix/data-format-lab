# Online Retail II

This directory contains a four-row fixture for the UCI Online Retail II contract. The full source is the UCI dataset 502 archive; its two sheets contain 1,067,371 rows and eight native columns. The fixture is not a substitute for the immutable full snapshot.

Source: [UCI archive](https://archive.ics.uci.edu/static/public/502/online%2Bretail%2Bii.zip), DOI `10.24432/C5CG6D`, CC BY 4.0. The manifest records observed archive and XLSX member hashes, the concatenation rule, and missing-value policy.
